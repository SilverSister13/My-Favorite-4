1. Animal switcheroo

This is a story about two animals, Kiwi the parrot, and Cookie the giraffe. They both envy one another thinking how great it must be to be able to fly of have such a long neck. While at the an animal festival dedicated to the gods, their bodies are switched as a lesson to be grateful. At the end their bodies are switched back and they both and learn to appreciate themselves.


2. A Forest Canopy

This story story is one I wrote many years ago, it is a bout two boys and their mother going into the forest canopy to study different animals, when one of them almost falls but is saved by a giant parrot. He later becomes friends with parrot, and one day when the parrot is in trouble, stuck in a net, the boy rescues him. This story gives the moral of "be kind, and the favor will be repaid".
